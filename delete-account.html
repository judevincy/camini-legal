Last updated: June 18, 2026
You can request deletion of your Camini account and all associated data at any time. Follow the steps below to submit your request directly from the app.

How to delete your account

  1. 1
    Open the Camini app on your device
  2. 2
    You'll land on your Profile page after signing in
  3. 3
    Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. 4
    This page opens — tap Email Us to Delete Account below to send your request
  5. 5
    We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days

What data is deleted

  • Deleted immediately: Your account credentials (email and password) in Firebase Authentication, your profile document (name, email, country) in Firestore, and your profile photo in Firebase Storage
  • Deleted within 30 days: Any usage analytics associated with your account
  • Not deleted: Anonymous, aggregated analytics that cannot be linked back to you

Partial data deletion

  • If you would like to delete only specific data (such as your profile photo or analytics) without deleting your full account, contact us at the email below and we will process your request within 30 days.

Can't access the app or need help with your request? Email us directly.
