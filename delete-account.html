Delete Your Camini Account

You can request deletion of your Camini account and all associated data by following the steps below.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Camini app on your device
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top-left corner
  3. Tap Sign Out
  4. Then send a deletion request to us by email (see below)

To complete your account deletion, email us with the subject line "Delete my account" and include the email address associated with your Camini account.

Email us to delete your account

What data is deleted

  • Deleted immediately: Your account credentials (email and password) held in Firebase Authentication
  • Deleted within 30 days: Any usage analytics associated with your account
  • Not deleted: Anonymous, aggregated analytics that cannot be linked back to you

Partial data deletion

If you would like to delete only specific data (such as analytics) without deleting your account, email us at jesus1288@gmail.com and we will process your request within 30 days.
