Privacy Policy

Camini — AI photo art app

Last updated: June 25, 2026
Camini ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered photo art app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect (including your name, email address, and country), how we use it, and your rights as a user.
1

Information We Collect

  • Full name — collected when you create an account, used to personalise your experience.
  • Email address — collected at sign-up, used for authentication and account-related communications.
  • Country — collected at sign-up to comply with regional data protection laws and to provide the correct subscription pricing.
  • Profile photo — if you choose to upload one, your profile picture is stored securely in Google Firebase Storage and displayed only to you within the app.
  • Photos for transformation — images you choose to transform are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. We do not store these photos on our servers.
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics (e.g. which styles are used, app crashes) collected via Google Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app.
  • Purchase information — subscription status managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details.
2

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account (name, email)
  • To personalise your in-app experience (name)
  • To determine the correct subscription pricing and comply with regional data protection law (country)
  • To send account-related communications such as email verification and password resets (email)
  • To display your profile picture within the app (profile photo, stored in Firebase Storage)
  • To process your photos using AI (via Google Gemini API)
  • To manage your subscription and billing (via RevenueCat)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs (anonymous analytics)

4

Data Retention

Your account data (name, email, country) and profile photo are retained while your account is active. Photos you transform are not stored on our servers after processing. Anonymous analytics data may be retained for up to 26 months. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

5

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own, including the United States, where our third-party service providers (Google Firebase, RevenueCat) operate. These transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards in accordance with applicable data protection law.

6

Children's Privacy

Camini is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on our legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach out — we're happy to help.
