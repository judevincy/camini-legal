Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Camini ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered photo art app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address — collected when you create an account, used only for authentication.
  • Photos — images you choose to transform are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. We do not store your photos on our servers.
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics (e.g. which styles are used, app crashes) collected via Google Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app.
  • Purchase information — subscription status managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Camini service
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To process your photos using AI (via Google Gemini API)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Services

Camini uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and analytics
  • Google Gemini API — AI image processing
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google Play — app distribution and payments

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Camini is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
